---

|| Ingredients |
|-:|-|
⅓ C   | butter, melted
3-4   | bananas
1 C   | sugar
1     | egg
1 tsp | vanilla
1 tsp | baking soda
pinch | salt
1½ C  | flour

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in order.
2. Bake at 350 for 1 to 1.5 hours.
