|| Ingredients |
|-:|-|
6 Tbsp | salted butter
2½ oz  | semi-sweet chocolate chips
1¼ C   | sugar
2      | eggs
1 tsp  | vanilla
&nbsp; | dash of salt
1      | pie crust (unbaked)

## Directions

1. Melt butter and chocolate in microwave.
2. Beat eggs.
3. Mix in sugar, vanilla, salt.
4. Mix in melted chocolate.
5. Bake in pie crust for 35-45 minutes at 350 degrees.
