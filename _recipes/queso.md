|| Ingredients |
|-:|-|
1 lb   | ground beef
1 C    | velveeta, cubed
½ C    | monterey jack, shredded
¼ C    | salsa
½ C    | onion, chopped
1 can  | green chilies, drained (4oz)
½ C    | tomatoes, diced
2 Tbsp | jalapenos, chopped
¼ C    | cilantro
1 tsp  | chili powder
½ tsp  | ground cumin
½ tsp  | dried oregano
¼ tsp  | red pepper flakes

## Directions

1. Cook beef and onion with salt and pepper in oil (7-9 min). Drain grease.
2. Stir in spices.
3. Stir in tomators, chilies, salsa.
4. Stir in cheeses, until melted (3-4 min).
5. Remove from heat and add jalapeno and cilantro.
