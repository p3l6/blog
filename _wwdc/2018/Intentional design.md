---
title: Intentional design
year: 2018
---
Remove extra UI, just have only what's needed
Colorful, animated, fun
Dig past superficial needs
Dont use 'home' as a tab bar item, specify what is on the tab. Home is for for getting out of a large web of options.
Draw on what people learn in the platform

5 elements of intent:
* Radical simplification
* Deep understanding
* Extremem focus
* Personal connection
* Direct communication
