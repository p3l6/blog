###  Today’s focus is apple’s services

* Apple news
  * Adding Magazines to News
  * Apple News+
  * More than 300 Magazines
  * Live covers (Magazine cover gifs)
  * Some newspapers. La Times, WSJ, etc
  * Advertiser privacy, no tracking
  * $9.99 per month (includes family sharing)
  * Available today, first month free
  * US and Canada so far
* Apple Pay
  * Adding US transit systems, select cities
* Apple Card (credit card)
  * Sign up on iPhone
  * Use where apple pay accepted
  * New capabilities in wallet to show usage, bills
  * Statement improvements
    * Remove cryptic transaction store names
    * Geo location
    * Categorization, spending habit tracking and trends
  * Cash rewards, not points. applied daily. added to apple cash card, **2%**
  * 3% rewards on apple products
  * Reward points are not limited
  * Emphasis on full payment, not minimum payment
  * No Fees (late, annual, international, over-limit)
  * Low interest rates. No penaltys for missing payments
  * Partnered with Goldman Sachs and MasterCard
  * Per-device card numbers
  * Privacy: Apple doesn’t know what you bought. GS will not share any data to third parties
  * **Comes with a physical card** for when apple pay is not accepted
    * Titanium, laser etched. (3D Render looks amazing)
    * Chip only, no card number, expiration, or signature (Info is in wallet app)
    * 1% rewards for physical card
  * Available this summer
* Apple Arcade
  * Game subscription service for mobile/desktop/tv
  * Over 100 new and exclusive games so far
  * New tab on the app store
  * Every game playable offline, on every type of device
  * No ads or additional subscriptions or purchase
  * Family sharing included
  * Available this fall, pricing TDB
* Apple TV
  * Bundle everthing in the new apple tv app
  * Apple TV Channels: Only pay for the channels you want
  * Ad free, offline availible
  * Family sharing
  * Siri and reccommendations
  * Available in May
  * Coming to the Mac in Fall
  * Coming to smart TVs, rollout by brand over the year
* Apple TV+
  * Original Apple content
  * Short presentations from selected shows:
    * Some guy not sure | Sci-Fi stories
    * Reese Witherspoon and Jennifer Aniston and Steve Carrell | Sitcom about a morining show
    * Aquaman dude and someone else | All characters are blind
    * Kamail nanjiani | True stories of immigrants in the US
    * Helpster from Sesame street | Coding for kids show
    * Sara Barrelles | Sitcom about a singer
    * Oprah | Documentaries
  * Coming this Fall
  * No mention of pricing
