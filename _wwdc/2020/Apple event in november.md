"There's just one more thing... Mac"
The next generation of Mac: Apple silicon

## M1
* First apple silicon chip
* System-on-Chip. A single chip that includes multiple standard components in one. GPU, memory, etc
* 8-core, split between high performance and high efficiency
* Great performance per watt compared to standard chips
* Integrated graphics
* Neural engine, Secure Enclave, etc

## Big Sur for M1
* has been designed for M1, to take advantage of hardware features
* Instant wake from sleep
* Apps
  * Universal apps, many coming soon
  * Rosetta2
  * Ipad apps on the mac

## Macbook Air
* The first mac with apple silicon
* Air is the most popular mac, apparently
* Better performace all around, obviously
* Silent! **No Fan**
* 6 Hour longer battery life than previous model
* Touch ID
* Same price! Starts at $999

## Mac mini
* I guess the "second" mac with apple silicon?
* Much faster, especially for graphics and machine learning
* Starts at $699 (lower than previous gen!)

## Macbook Pro 13 inch
* They're announcing more macs than I expected!
* Real escape key, touch bar
* Faster, etc
* 10 More hours of battery life than before. Longest battery life ever in a mac
* Same price, $1299

Order new macs today, available next week
