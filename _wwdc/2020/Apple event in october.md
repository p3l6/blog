## Homepod mini
* Sphere instead of cylinder
* Sound, Siri, Smart home hub, privacy
* Multiple speakers can stay perfectly in sync
* Or become a stereo pair if in the same room
* With new iphones, they'll know if you hold it nearby
* Siri can recognize voice of each house member
* Intercom, send messages between homepods (and iphones, watch, carplay)
* White and gray, $99, available Nov 6

## iPhone 12
* All models have 5G network support
* Flat edge square design!
* Five colors: gray, white, red, green, blue
* OLED display
* New tougher display glass: ceramic shield
* Smart data mode: switch between LTE and 5G automatically to save power
* Game: League of legends coming to iPhone 12. Because its like, fast, or whatever
* Cameras, or whatever
* **Magsafe** for iPhone
  * Aligns wireless charger
  * Phone cases snap on
  * Magsafe wallet snaps to the back
  * Magsafe duo charger for iphone and watch, folds for travel
* Will not come with headphones or power adapter. Usbc - lightning cable
* Starts at $799

### iPhone 12 mini
* Smaller than iphone 8, with larger screen
* All the same features as iphone 12
* Starts at $699, available Nov 6

### iPhone 12 pro line
* Premium materials of the standard design. Stanless steel.
* Slightly bigger displays for both
* Lots of cameras and photo features
* LiDAR scanner. For room/object scanning and AR
* Starts at $999, Max $1099
