## Apple watch
* watchOS 7
  * handwashing detection
  * sleep tacking
  * can measure "vO2max"
* Series 6
* New colors: blue alum case, red, gold, black
* New health sensor: blood oxygen level.
  * take a measurement in 15 sec
  * background measurements as well
  * New research studies about this data
* S6 chip system, faster etc
  * Better always on display 2.5x brighter outdoors
  * Always on altimiter
* New watch faces
  * GMT, countup, chronograph pro, typeograph, art, memoji, stripes
* New watch bands
  * Solo loop: No buckle, stretches, colors.
  * Braided yarn version too
  * Leather loop
* Family setup: Use your iphone to pair watches for children/elderly. Without them needing a phone. (requires cellular model). Tracking, "School time", etc
* **Apple watch SE**
  * Like series 6, but with S5 chip
  * Big stlye screens
* Pricing Starts at:  SE $279 / series 6 $399 / series 3 $199
* Removing the usb power adapter from watch. For eco waste reasons.
