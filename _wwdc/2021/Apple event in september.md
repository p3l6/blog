## Apple TV+
* Foundation
* Swagger
* Finch
* The shrink next door

## iPad (standard)
* A13 bionic
* Front camera upgrade, with center stage
* True tone
* Supports newer ipad keyboards
* Starts at $329

## iPad mini
* Design aesthetics of the ipad air and pro
  * Narrow borders
  * Camera bump
  * New colors
  * Touch id home button like air
  * UsbC
* 8.3 inch screen
* 5G cellular
* Smart folio style like air
* Apple pencil 2
* Starts at $499

## Apple watch series 7
* WatchOS 8, with cycling fitness updates
* New display
  * 20% larger screen, with thinner borders
  * Softer more rounded corners
  * Brighter
* Full keyboard, with quickpath
* New watch faces for series 7
* Improved dust resistence
* Faster charging, with usbc charger
* Five new colors
* New band colors
* Starts at $399. Series 3 and SE stay in lineup at existing prices

## Fitness+
* New courses
  * Pilates
  * Guided meditation
  * Workout series to get ready for ski season
* Group workouts with shareplay and facetime

## iPhone 13
* Double camera bump
  * Diagonal dual lenses
  * Color stabilization from iphone 12 pro max
  * Cinematic video mode, automatic focus changes
* Five new colors
* Narrower notch
* Two sizes, standard and mini
* A15 bionic chip
* Better battery life
* Magsafe
  * New colors and cases
  * Wallet has Find My support
* Still starts at $699 for mini, $799 standard
  * Starting capacity 128gb

## iPhone 13 Pro
* Three cameras still
* Narrower notch
* Two sizes, pro and max
* Pro motion display
  * Adaptable framerate between 10 and 120 hz
  * Auto adjusts based on content
* Fancy camera stuff

iPhone 12 still in lineup, now starting at $599
