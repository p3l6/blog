## iOS 15
* FaceTime
  * Spatial audio
  * Voice isolation (disableable)
  * Grid view
  * Portriat mode, blurs background
  * Facetime links, generate and share to invite.
    * Join via browser, for non-apple devices
  * SharePlay
    * Bring in music and movies into a facetime call
    * Share screen
    * Stuff will stay in sync
    * API for developers to integrate with
* Messages
  * Picture collages/stacks
  * Shared with you
    * News, photos, music apps will highlight things shared with you
    * Safari, tv, podcasts as well.
* Notifications
  * Summary
  * When DnD is on, imessage users will see you are busy
  * Focus
    * Can choose a "mode", allow apps and contacts you want to use
    * Ie, personal, work, sleep
    * Customized home screens for each
    * Can suggest focus, ie "gym", based on location
    * Syncs across devices
* Live text
  * Select text from camera, or existing photos
  * Copy, look up, call, etc
  * Visual look up, breed of dog, type of flower, landmarks
  * Photos search from spotlight
* Photos memories
  * Brings music into memories
  * Adjust mixes and themes
* Wallet
  * Tap to unlock your house, workplace, hotel rooms
  * Identity cards, scan your drivers license (participating states)
    * TSA will be the first place that you can use this securely
* Weather: more data points, widgets, and maps
* Maps
  * Interactive globe
  * In a few major cities
    * New landmarks, elevations, trees, parks etc
    * Driving has more road details, crosswalks, lanes, lines, highway interchanges
    * Transit improved
    * Augmentented reality navigation
* Airpods
  * Conversation boost, ie hearing aid mode
  * Improved siri reading messages
  * Pro and max pods will work with find my

* Widgets and app library, just like iphone last year
  * Library will be in the dock
* Multitasking menu
  * Buttons to put apps side by side
  * Minimizing on "the shelf" ie all open windows of an app
  * New keyboard shortcuts
* Notes
  * Mentions, Activity, Tags
  * Quick note
    * Swipe from corner to jot down a quick note
    * Context aware
* Translate coming to iPad
  * Practice handwriting in another language with pencil
  * Side by side with webpages
  * System wide, select and translate, even in "live text"
* Swift playgrounds
  * ==Build apps on iPad==!
  * With SwiftUI
  * Run full screen,
  * Submit to app store

## Privacy
* Mail privacy protection
  * Hides ip and location
  * Prevents open email notifications
* App privacy report
  * Reports how often apps have used permissions you've granted
* Siri
  * Many new voice options

    * Make many requests without internet
    * Faster

## MacOS Monterey
* Many cross features from things above
* Universal control
  * Single mouse and keyboard swaps between mac and ipad
  * Just by setting ipad next to laptop
  * Drag and drop between devices
  * Even more than two devices, all link together
* Airplay to Mac, from other devices
* Shortcuts, just like for iphone coming to mac
  * Looks like modernized automator
  * Import from automator. automator still supported (for now)
* Safari
  * Completely new tab design
  * Integrated tab bar and search bar
  * Tab groups, save tabs and get back to them
  * Most buttons moved to the dropdown menu
  * (Also on ipad, iphone on bottom)
  * Extensions coming to ipad/iphone
