## iPhone SE 3

* Same basic form factor as SE 2

## iPad Air
* M1 chip
* Ultrawide front camera, for center stage
* 5G

## Mac Studio & Studio Display
* New chip: M1 ultra.
  * Completes the top-end of the range: M1, pro, max, ultra
  * M1 max secret feature: die-to-die connection strip. "UltraFusion"
  * Basically connects two chips without hitting the motherboard
  * 800 GB/s memory bandwidth
  * Up to 128 GB memory
* Mac Studio
  * Replaces the Mac Pro, I suppose
  * Looks like a taller mac mini
  * Two thunderbolt4, sd card ports on the front
  * Four thunderbolt4, ethernet, two usb, hdmi, and a pro audio port on the back
  * Dual fans pull air through base, and out the back
  * Can run up to 4 proDisplays, and a TV at once
  * Configured with either M1 max, or M1 ultra
  * Starts at $1999, $3999 for ultra
* Studio Display
  * Slim profile, similar to current iMac
  * Multiple stand options
  * 27" active screen area
  * Onboard A13 chip, powering camera and audio system
  * Three usbc ports, and a thunderbolt port to connect with a single cable
  * Works with all the macs
  * Starts at $1599
* New silver & black combo keyboard/mouse/trackpad colors
