---
title: Whats new in xcode
year: 2022
---
* 30% Smaller, download additional platform sdks on demand
* Interactive swiftui previews
* Buttons to create swiftui variations, without code for each
* SFsymbols in library
* Default init and codable init snippets
* Better completion for methods with optional args. Fuzzy match the args you need.
* Problems from a previous build are dimmed grey
* Callers and Jump to show a preview of what's going on there
* Build timeline inspector
* 4x faster notarization
* Better leak inspector
* Swift package plugins
* Feedback and hangs reports in organizer
