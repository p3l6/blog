## Recap
* New macs introduced earlier this year
  * Transition to apple silicon is complete
* Vision pro announced
## Apple watch series 9
* Precision iphone finding
* Media suggestions when close to homepod
* New gesture, "double tap", tapping your thumb and and finger
  * Activates primary button
* Reducing and removing leather in all lines
## Apple watch ultra 2
* Brighter
* Night mode
* Customizable action button
* 95% recycled titanium
## iPhone 15
* Dynamic island
* Countoured edge
* Camera stuff
* Noise reduction on calls
* Roadside assistance via satelite
  * Built on the same platform as Sat Emergency
* UsbC. no more lightning
* New "fine woven" cases to replace leather
## iPhone 15 Pro
* Titanium enclosure. lighter and thinner.
* Action button
  * Replaces the ring silent switch
  * Press and hold to choose behavior.
  * Can choose other actions instead, for single press, long press, or double.
* A17 Pro chip
  * Ray tracing graphics in hardware
* New games that were only available on console
* Camera improvements
  * Adjustable focal length
  * Record video directly to external drives
  * Record spacial videos for vision pro
* All phones available next week
* Two new icloud pluss plans, 6 and 12 TB
