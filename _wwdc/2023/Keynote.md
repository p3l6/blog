## Apple Vision Pro
* Classic "One more thing..."
* Augmented reality device
* Looks like funny scuba goggles
* Can jump into an environment, more like a VR landscape, or use it as the backgroud for an app
* Uses eyes, hands, and voice for control
* Browse by looking, tap fingers to select, or flick to scroll
* Speak to dictate in a text field, or virtual keyboard
* Displays your eyes on the outside of the device, to help others notice you can see them
* Also works with bluetooth, keyboards and trackpads.
* Or even mac, using vision as an external display
* Facetime was discussed, but they glossed over what the vision user looks like to others
  * They got back to this, you scan your face at the beginning. and creates an AI generated version of yourself
* Can capture 3d spatial video and play it back
* Movies and 3d movies too. Can make them appear projected on a big screen far away.
* Apple arcade games with controller support.
* Separate, sizeable, light blockers and headbands
* Uses M2, and new chip, R1 for realtime sensor processing
* visionOS, a whole new platform. new app store, some new frameworks, new targets
* Using existing frameworks like ARKit, RealityKit, etc.
* New framework for reality composer
* Optic id, like face id, but for your eyes.
* Apps cannot track where you are looking, only when you click your fingers.
* Starts at $3499, available "early next year"
