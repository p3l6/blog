## iPad air
* Two sizes, standard 11 and new 13
* Front facing camera on the side, for better facetime angles
* Landscape stereo speakers
* M2 and 128GB base storage
* Still starts at $599

## iPad pro
* "Impossibly thin" 5.3 mm, thinner than ipad nano.
* Also 11 and 13 in sizes
* OLED display with two panels "Tandem OLED". "Ultra retina XDR"
* M4, first device with this processor
* Front camera on landscape side
  * Face id
* New magic keyboard
  * Thinner
  * Function row
  * Starts at $299
* Starts at $999

## Apple pencil pro
* Squeeze sensor to select tools
* Gyro sensor to detect rolling, ie for a shaped brush
* Supports find my
* Haptic feedback
* Works with both new ipad lines
* Still starts at $129

## Apps
* Updates to final cut pro
  * Iphone companion app: final cut camera
* Updates to logic pro
  * Some new AI players, stem splitting, etc
