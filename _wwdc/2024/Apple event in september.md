year: 2024

## Apple Watch S10
* Bigger display, 1hz refresh when idle. Thinner in exchange.
* Titanium finish replaces stainless steel
* Sleep apnea detection
* Water depth gauge and water temperature
* New app to track tides, kayak info added to workouts
## AirPods 4
* Nod or shake to indicate to siri
* UsbC charging case
* Optional upgrade with Active noise cancellation, from pro line, and transparency mode
* AirPods Max, new colors and UsbC
* AirPods Pro, hearing protection, hearing test, hearing aid mode.
  * Boost specific sounds (based on the hearing test) in real time
## iPhone 16
* Action button
* Only 2 cameras, smaller bump
* New camera control button. With a slider. It is depressed, unlike the other buttons.
  * launches camera, or takes a picture
  * half press to get zoom, etc
* Visual intelligence, search or take actions about objects on the camera
