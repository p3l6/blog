## Airpods Pro 3
* New airflow paths
* Better active noise cancellation & foam infused tips
* Active translation, by phrase
  * Audio for you, text on phone for others, unless everyone has airpods
* Smaller, to fit more ears, with 5 tip sizes
* ip57 sweat and water resistance
* Heart rate sensor in the earpod
  * Track workouts in fitness app with just airpods
* Better battery life
* Same price at $249

## Apple watch series 11
* 2x more scratch resistant than series 10
* 5g cellular
* High blood pressure detection
  * Reports possible hypertension
* Sleep score
  * Based on three categories, time asleep, wakeups, bedtime consistency
* Up to 24 hrs battery
* Basic 4 colors
* $399
### Apple watch SE 3
* Always on display
* Gestures like wrist flick and double tap
* Better vitals, sleep score, etc
* 18hr battery
* Fast charging
* 2 colors
* $249
### Apple watch ultra 3
* Wide angle OLED screen, largest display
* Sattelite connectivity
* 100% recycled titanium
* $799

## iPhone 17
* 5 pastel colors
* 6.3" ProMotion display, smaller bezels, always on
* Better scratch resistance with ceramic shield 2
* Chip A19
  * New display engine
  * Bigger neural engine
* Two cameras (down from 3? - thats good) Fusion & Telephoto
  * Still a double bump
* Center stage front camera
  * Square sensor, to take landscape without turning
* Camera control
* 256 gb base storage
* $799
