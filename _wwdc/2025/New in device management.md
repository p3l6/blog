---
title: What's new in device management and identity
year: 2025
---

* Services (Apple business manager)
  * Sign in with federated authentication
  * Companies can take ownership of accounts on their domain
  * Can ensure only work accounts can log into work devices
  * New apis for viewing device information
  * Device management migration
* Device management
  * Automatic forced updates coming to vision and tv
  * Manage bookmarks and homepage in safari
  * Improved speed in "return to service". and added to vision
* App management
  * Manage update behavior on per-app basis
  * Pin apps to specific versions
  * Apps can configure themselves to work with app management passwords, keys, etc
* Identity integrations
  * Platform SSO will happen earlier, during setup
  * Authenticated guest mode - login with cloud identity
    * Tap to login with phone. ie hospital style login
