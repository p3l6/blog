---
title: Principles of Great Design
year: 2026
---

* Making something with intention. Does it have ==purpose==?
* Offer choices to give ==agency==.
* ==Responsibility==:
  * Avoid disaster, allow undo.
  * Privacy, ask for permission when appropriate
  * Keep people safe, who could be harmed by this?
* Build on what people know, make if ==familiar==. Use the common icons.
* Consistency is key, but ==flexibility== is important.
* Make it ==simple==, but this is not the same as minimal.
  * Concise language
  * Avoid redundancy
  * Good hierarchy
* Care about all the details (==Craft==)
  * Colors, icons, layout, responsivity
* ==Delight==
