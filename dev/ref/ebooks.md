## Convert kindle to nook  <!-- Section -->

1. Check "epubor kcr converter" trial (trial tracker might be `~/.KCR/config` ?)
2. Close "epubor kcr converter"
3. Buy book on amazon, deliver to kindle cloud reader
4. Open chrome with kindle cloud reader app installed
5. Right click on new book, download and pin
6. Open "epubor kcr converter" again, close license window
7. Select book, convert to epub
8. Open output (folder is `~/KCR/`) in calibre
9. Connect Nook (use a data cable), send to device
